Galactic Center Density

  • Likely $\sim 10^4$ BHs in central pc
  • Mass segregation (e.g. Morris 1993) or in-situ formation in disk (Generozov et al 2018)
  • Here $m_*(a)=6.7\times 10^4 \left(\frac{a}{0.1 {\rm pc}}\right)^{1.25} M_{\odot}$ and $m_{\rm bh}(a)=2.4\times 10^4 \left(\frac{a}{0.1 {\rm pc}}\right) M_{\odot}$ (Antonini&Merritt 2013)

Conclusions

  • Binary disruption + Relaxation can explain observed S-stars (similar to Antonini&Merritt2013).
  • Requires ~1000 black holes within central 0.1 pc.
